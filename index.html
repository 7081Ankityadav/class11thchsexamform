Entrance Test Instructions

  1. The candidate must check the Question Paper Booklet within 10 minutes after it is issued and ensure that it contains all the pages and that no page is missing. If any page is missing, the candidate should immediately report to the invigilator and get it replaced with a complete Question Paper Booklet.

  2. The candidate must write the Roll Number only on the Cover Page and on the Answer Sheet of the Question Paper at the places provided for the purpose and nowhere else. Otherwise, the candidate will be disqualified from the Entrance Test.

  3. There shall be no erasing or overwriting in the answers given on the Answer Sheet. If any erasing or overwriting is found, such answers will be treated as incorrect.

  4. Use of log tables or calculators is not permitted.

  5. No candidate will be permitted to enter the Examination Hall after 20 minutes from the commencement of the Entrance Test.

  6. No candidate will be permitted to leave the Examination Hall before two hours from the commencement of the Entrance Test.

  7. Candidates are not allowed to bring any loose paper, whether written or blank, inside the Examination Hall except the Admit Card.

  8. Candidates using any kind of unfair means or attempting to create disturbance in the Examination Hall will be expelled from the Examination and their performance in the test will be cancelled.

  9. Candidates must submit the OMR Answer Sheet to the invigilator at the end of the examination.
